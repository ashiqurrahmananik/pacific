About Pacific

In 2004 Pacific Chemical started manufacturing and marketing Garments Spot Remover (GSR) products for the first time in Bangladesh.Till now we are the best among all GSR manufacturing company in Bangladesh with 1500+ satisfied customers nationwide.

Pacific Chemical invented 14+ versatile products in own R & D laboratory and committed to manufacture more products to this industry.Our products has made a great contribution to the garments industry by manufacturing remover chemicals.
